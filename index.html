About me

Hello,

My name is Firenzo Jorden. For now I am a student, studying Communication and Multimedia Design at Hogeschool van Amsterdam (HvA). In this course I like Visual design and Front end development the most. When designing something, I always keep the user in mind, because I know how frustrating it can be if an application doesn't fit your needs while you expected it to do so.

My main hobby is playing games. Ever since I was a kid I couldn't get enough of games. I've done a minor in game design as well. we learned the basics of game theory and game art. We also got to do a few projects where we could make our own game, which I really enjoyed!

Besides gaming I like to produce music. I listen to hip-hop a lot, so that's also what I (try to) produce the most. I'm hoping to become really good at it someday.

Then there is video editting. This is something I started to like more and more as we had to make video's for certain excersices in school. I would love to get the opportunity to make more (professional) video's someday.
